## Part 5: Development Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation
**Duration:** 8 weeks

| Week | Focus | Deliverables |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1-2 | IBKR Integration | TWS connection, account sync, position sync |
| 3-4 | Income Sleeve | Cosmos DB, drift calculator, quality gates, buy list, orders |
| 5-6 | Tactical Sleeve | Technical indicators, breakout/pullback scans, Polygon.io |
| 7-8 | Risk & Reporting | Risk validation, stops, emergency halt, Discord reports |

### Phase 2: AI & Options
**Duration:** 6 weeks

| Week | Focus | Deliverables |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 9-10 | Claude AI | Market regime analysis, candidate ranking, fallback handling |
| 11-12 | Income Audits | Quarterly audit, quality assessment, reduction signals |
| 13-14 | Options | IV calculation, CSP, bear call spreads, covered calls |

### Phase 3: Validation & Go-Live
**Duration:** 14+ weeks

| Week | Focus | Deliverables |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 15-26 | Paper Trading | 12+ weeks validation, performance analysis, tuning |
| 27-28 | Live Transition | Fund account, configure live, pilot period |
